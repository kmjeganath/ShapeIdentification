It is a Rectangle

Rectangles are quadrilateral. For a rectangle, two parallel and opposite sides are equal in length but 4 angles are same. When divided diagonally, the triangles that form in a rectangle are called as the isosceles triangles. A rectangle is always flatter in shape with four distinct points.

It is a Square

A square is a polygon with 4 sides of equal length and 4 right angle corners. Because it has 4 sides of equal length, a square is a regular quadrilateral. The diagonals of a square bisect each other at 90 degrees and are perpendicular. The internal angles of a square add to 360 degrees.

It is a Triangle

Triangles are polygons with the least possible number of sides (three). The three internal angles of a triangle always add to 180 degrees. The longest side of a right angle triangle is called the hypotenuse, it is always found opposite the right angle. Various types of triangles are equilateral, isosceles, scalene, right angle, obtuse and acute triangle.

It is a Pentagon

A pentagon is a 5 sided two dimensional polygon with interior angles that add to 540 degrees. If all sides are equal and all angles are equal, then pentagon is regular, otherwise it is irregular. A regular pentagon has interior angles of 108 degrees and exterior angles is of 72 degrees. An equilateral pentagon is a polygon with five sides of equal length.

It is an Ellipse

Ellipses are like circles which have been squashed or stretched. They have 2 lines of symmetry. The longest and shortest diameters of the ellipse are called the major and minor axes. These axes are also the lines of symmetry.

It is a Circle

Circles have a point in the centre from which each point on the diameter is equidistant. A straight line from the center of a circle to the edge is called the radius. A straight line that passes from one side of a circle to the other through the center is called the diameter. They have infinite lines of symmetry.

It is a Cylinder

A cylinder is a three dimensional object with a circular base and straight parallel sides. The base is the same as the top, and also in-between. It has one curved side. It is not a polyhedron as it has a curved surface.

It is a Crescent

Crescent shapes are made when two circles overlap, or when one circle is removed from another circle . The perimeter of crescents are made from two circular arcs. They have 1 line of symmetry. Our moon forms crescent shapes during its phases.
